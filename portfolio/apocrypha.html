Apocrypha

Apocrypha is my latest solo-project, a 3D space shooter I started developing shortly after releasing Solo Wing.
I really liked the end result of Solo Wing and I wanted to continue developing the idea.

Development started in late November 2020, and as of March 2021 there's an Alpha Demo available to download.

Development went to a halt as I started working full-time since then.
